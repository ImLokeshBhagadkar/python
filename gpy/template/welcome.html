Welcome to Government Polytechnic Yavatmal
(Formerly known as Govt. Residential Women's Polytechnic, Yavatmal )



Vision :

To be a source of techno-functional manpower to meet the needs of industries and society.

Mission :

To provide technically sound, quality conscious and environmentally concerned human resources to serve society through life-long learning.
To develop leadership qualities, supervisory skills and entrepreneurial attitude to enhance employability.
To produce competent engineers by providing ethical values and excellent technical education

About us

Government Residential Women's Polytechnic, Yavatmal was established in 1994 with a primary aim to impart Technical Education exclusively to girls. From Jun 2018, the institute is known as Government Polytechnic Yavatmal with the admissions open for both boys and girls.
